# Bimbo Electronics Mart Sales Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This project provides a detailed **Excel-based sales analysis** of Bimbo Electronics Mart. It demonstrates the ability to clean data, analyze sales performance, and visualize insights using dashboards and charts. The analysis focuses on **product revenue, total revenue per product, and best-selling products**.

---

## 🔹 Key Metrics & Insights
- **Top-selling product by revenue:** Macbook Pro Laptop — $773,500 across 455 units sold.
- **Highest volume product:** AAA Batteries (4-pack) — 2,933 units ordered, reflecting strong demand for low-cost accessories.
- **Revenue gap:** The Macbook Pro generated over 89x more revenue than the lowest earner (AAA Batteries at $8,769), highlighting a significant spread between high-ticket and low-ticket items.
- **Best performing month:** April recorded $3,384,047.56 in total revenue — significantly outperforming May.

**Sales Insights:**  
- Identifies top-performing products and revenue contributors.  
- Highlights areas where product performance can be improved.  
- Provides insights into the effectiveness of different sales channels.

**Recommendations:**  
- Focus marketing and promotions on **best-selling products** to maximize revenue.  
- Consider strategies to **boost underperforming products**.  
- Optimize inventory and stock levels based on product performance trends.
